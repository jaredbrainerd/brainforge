02 — About

Design is not decoration. It is vision made real.

I'm a product designer and creative director obsessed with the collision of maximalist aesthetics and systems thinking. Based in Denver, working globally — with brands, studios, and mavericks who want work that actually means something.

I believe in excess with intent. Every element earns its place, even if there are a hundred of them.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me somewhere outside — hiking a trail, chasing a summit, or just breathing in the Colorado air. Nature has a way of resetting the mind and sharpening the eye. The best ideas tend to show up when you're far from a screen.
